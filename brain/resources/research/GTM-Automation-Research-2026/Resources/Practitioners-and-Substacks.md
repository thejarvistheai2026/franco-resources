| Handle | Focus | Why Follow |

| **@C17Lab (Enzo Carasso)** | Extreme-volume outbound | Cold email infrastructure at scale. Posts detailed workflow breakdowns with real numbers. |

### Why These Matter

**Joe Rhew** — If you want to see what "terminal-first GTM" actually looks like in practice. Not theoretical — he shows the code, the connections, the failures.

**Enzo Carasso** — Volume infrastructure is a different beast than one-off workflows. His breakdowns cover deliverability, warming, domain rotation — the unsexy stuff that makes or breaks campaigns.

**Max Mitcham** — Social intent is underutilized. Most people scrape job changes; he's pulling signal from LinkedIn activity (comments, posts, engagement) that indicate buying intent *before* the job change.

**Eric Nowoslawski** — Case studies with real numbers from recognizable companies. Rare combination of tactical depth + social proof.

**Arpit Singh** — Consistent output on Clay workflows. Good for staying current on new Clay features and combinations.

## 🎯 How to Actually Use This

### Weekly Ritual
1. **Monday:** Check GTM Engineer Pulse for new tool/feature alerts
2. **Tuesday-Thursday:** Browse one practitioner's X feed for workflow ideas
3. **Friday:** Skim The First Operator for strategic context

### Monthly Ritual
- **Search:** "your target workflow + @author" on X (e.g., "cold email @C17Lab")
- **Review:** Which workflows did they post in the last 30 days? Any new tools?
- **Steal:** Adapt one workflow to your stack

### Quarterly Ritual
- **Audit:** Which practitioners are still active? Who's gone quiet?
- **Update:** Prune this list, add new names
- **Deep Dive:** Read one practitioner's entire back catalog on a specific topic
