## 🎯 What is Apollo.io?

Apollo is a comprehensive sales engagement platform combining:
- **Database:** 275M+ verified B2B contacts
- **Data Intelligence:** Company info, technographics, buying signals
- **Engagement Tools:** Email sequences, calls, LinkedIn, task management
- **AI Features:** AI-assisted writing, automation, analytics

Unlike point solutions, Apollo aims to be the single platform for entire sales workflow.

## 🤖 AI Capabilities

### Current AI Features:
1. **AI Email Writer**
   - Analyzes prospect data
   - Generates personalized message
   - Learns from high-performing templates

2. **Send-Time Optimization**
   - Predicts best time to send
   - Based on prospect's past engagement
   - Increases open rates

3. **Reply Intent Detection**
   - Categorizes replies (interested, not interested, etc.)
   - Suggests next actions
   - Auto-pauses sequences on positive replies

### AI Limitations:
- Not fully autonomous (still co-pilot mode)
- Requires human review for best results
- Works best with good input data
