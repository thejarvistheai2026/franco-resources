## 🎯 What is Clay?

Clay describes itself as a **"GTM Development Environment"** - essentially an IDE (Integrated Development Environment) for growth teams combined with a runtime environment where growth ideas can continuously execute.

### Core Concept
Traditional GTM tools solve single problems (find contacts, send emails, etc.). Clay combines:
- 100+ data source integrations
- AI agents for research
- Visual workflow builder
- Continuous execution environment

The result: Teams can turn any growth idea into reality in minutes instead of months.

## 🔑 Key Capabilities

### 1. Waterfall Data Enrichment
Clay's signature feature - cross-referencing 50-100+ data sources to find the best match for any data point.

**How it works:**
1. Query primary provider (e.g., Apollo)
2. If no match → query secondary (e.g., ZoomInfo)
3. If no match → query tertiary (e.g., Clearbit)
4. Continue cascading through providers
5. Final fallback: AI research agent

**Results:**
- Anthropic tripled enrichment rates vs. single provider
- ~500 hours/year saved on procurement (per enterprise buyer)
- No separate contracts needed - use Clay credits for any provider

**Supported Data Types:**
- Contact info (email, phone, LinkedIn)
- Firmographics (company size, revenue, industry)
- Technographics (tech stack, tools used)
- Intent signals (job changes, hiring, promotions)
- Custom data points via AI agents

### 2. Claygent (AI Research Agent)
Powered by OpenAI, the Claygent is an AI agent that performs research tasks at scale.

**Use Cases:**
- Scrape and summarize websites
- Find certifications and awards
- Analyze financial documents
- Assess email domain fraud risk
- Clean and format data
- Research specific custom criteria

**Scale:**
- 30% of Clay customers use Claygent daily
- 500,000+ research and outreach tasks per day
- Teams of 1-2 achieving what previously required dozens of engineers

**OpenAI Case Study Quote:**
> "The widespread adoption of Claygent has been a significant driver of this growth... The impact is undeniable — teams of one or two can achieve results that previously required dozens of engineers."

### 3. Intent Signals
Clay acquired Avenue in 2024 to add intent signal capabilities.

**Current Signals:**
- Job changes (new hires, promotions, departures)
- Hiring activity (job postings analysis)
- Coming soon: Funding rounds, tech stack changes, third-party intent

**Use Case:**
Automatically trigger outreach when:
- Target prospect gets promoted
- Prospect's company posts relevant job opening
- Key stakeholder changes companies

### 4. Data Activation
Once data is enriched, Clay activates it across the tech stack.

**Native Integrations:**
- CRMs: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive
- Email sequencers: Outreach, Salesloft, Apollo
- Data warehouses: Snowflake, BigQuery
- Ad platforms: LinkedIn, Facebook
- HTTP API for custom integrations

**Workflows Possible:**
- Auto-enrich inbound leads
- Trigger campaigns based on signals
- Generate meeting prep documents
- Launch competitive campaigns (monitor status pages, trigger on downtime)
- Personalized direct mail campaigns
- Custom landing pages
