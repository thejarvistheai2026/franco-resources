## 🎯 What is Unify GTM?

Unify GTM is a go-to-market technology platform leveraging AI and intent data to automate end-to-end sales intelligence and engagement workflows.

**Core Philosophy:** "Warm Outbound" - reaching out to prospects who have already shown intent signals, rather than cold outreach.

---

## 🔑 Key Capabilities

### 1. Signals & Data

**Data Aggregation:**
- Pulls from 10+ different data sources
- Real-time intent signal detection
- Buyer readiness indicators

**Intent Signals Tracked:**
- Website visits (with identification)
- Content engagement
- Email interactions
- Job changes
- Funding announcements
- Technology changes
- Hiring activity

**Benefit:** Know exactly when a prospect is actively looking to buy, allowing perfect timing for outreach.

### 2. Plays (Workflow Automation)

**What It Does:**
Automates repetitive GTM tasks through configurable workflows:
- Prospecting new leads
- Enriching existing data
- AI-driven research
- Outreach sequencing

**Visual Workflow Builder:**
- Drag-and-drop interface
- Trigger-based automation
- Conditional logic
- Multi-step sequences

### 3. Sequences

**Multi-Touch Campaigns:**
- Create multi-touch outreach campaigns
- Personalized messaging at scale
- Email deliverability management
- Multi-channel support (email, calls, social)

**Deliverability Features:**
- Warm-up protocols
- Sender reputation management
- Spam folder avoidance
- Domain health monitoring

### 4. AI Agents

**Capabilities:**
- Scale account research automatically
- Gather information from websites and search engines
- Analyze CRM data for insights
- Craft personalized messages
- Ensure communications resonate with each prospect

**How It Works:**
1. AI analyzes prospect's digital footprint
2. Researches company and persona
3. Generates personalized message draft
4. Sales rep reviews and sends

### 5. Warm Outbound Engine

**Differentiator:** Unify focuses on "warm" rather than cold outbound.

**Components:**
- Gradual sending volume ramp-up
- Trusted domain management
- High sender reputation maintenance
- Deliverability rate optimization

**Benefit:** Higher response rates, lower spam complaints, better brand reputation.

### 6. Analytics & Dashboards

**Reporting Areas:**
- Workflow performance metrics
- Pipeline development tracking
- Email engagement rates (opens, clicks, replies)
- Conversion analytics
- ROI measurement

**Dashboards:**
- Real-time performance views
- Historical trend analysis
- Team and individual metrics
- Custom report builder

## 🔧 Common Use Cases

### 1. Intent-Based Triggers
```
Website Visit Detected → Enrich Visitor Data → Score Lead → Trigger Personalized Email
```

**Workflow:**
- Prospect visits your website
- Unify identifies company and person
- Enriches with additional data
- Checks against ICP criteria
- Triggers personalized outreach

### 2. Third-Party Signal Response
```
Funding News Detected → Research Round Details → Find Decision Makers → Outreach Campaign
```

**Automated Actions:**
- Monitor news sources for funding announcements
- Enrich company data post-funding
- Identify likely budget holders
- Launch congratulatory + solution pitch sequence

### 3. Automated Outbound Campaigns
```
Define ICP → Auto-Prospect → Enrich → Sequence → Track → Optimize
```

**Scale:**
- Target thousands of accounts
- Personalized at individual level
- Automated follow-up
- Performance-based optimization

### 4. Account-Based Marketing (ABM)
```
Target Account List → Multi-Stakeholder Sequences → Engagement Tracking → Sales Handoff
```

**Features:**
- Account-level orchestration
- Multi-contact coordination
- Engagement scoring at account level
- Sales team notifications
