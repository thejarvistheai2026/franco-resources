- **Most innovation efforts fail because they focus on developing innovations without thinking through whether, and how, these will create growth and change.     - Organizations fail to disrupt because they fall short of fully committing to a strategy centered on serving future customers; they cut corners rather than take on the full brunt of the angst and pain this strategy will cause.
