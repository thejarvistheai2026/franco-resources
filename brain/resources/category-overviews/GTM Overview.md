- Step 1: Understand the USERS
    - What part of your USER’s workflow are you disrupting?
    - What is your business case?
    - Who are your USER, BUYER, Enabler, and Detractor?
    - Who is your competition?
        - list of direct and indirect competitors, then highlight the differences across both lists:
        - i)What aspects are unique differentiators (features only you provide)?
        - ii)What aspects are comparative differentiators (features you both provide, even if slightly different)?
        - iii)How are your pricing models similar/different?
        - iv)What are the holistic differentiators (i.e. why is the experience of working with your company superior? Have you been given any awards in your space? What customer testimonials do you have? etc)?
        - How are you going to package and price your product?
- Step 2: understand USER / BUYER journey
- Step 3: design your GTM funnel. Your funnel is a means of organizing your activities, metrics, and goals and aligning them with the appropriate steps of the USER/BUYER Journey.
- Step 4: develop your strategy for getting your product into the mix of solutions your USER is considering to solve their problem.
