#### Key Details

- Stop using OpenClaw like a search engine.
	- Build employees instead:
		- Give it a role (Developer, Researcher, Writer)
		- Give it a personality (Soul File)
		- Give it memory (daily logs)
		- Give it tools (API keys, skills)
	- One AI agent = assistant Multiple AI agents = company


- OpenClaw tends to default to human timelines when it comes to building. They often forget that they are operating in AI timelines. So ask it to give you three timelines: 
	- The AI timeline 
	- The human timeline 
	- The actual timeline (all-in)
