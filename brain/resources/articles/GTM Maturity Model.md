#### Key Details
- A best-in-class go-to-market engine has 4 key pillars:
    
    - [_Targeting:_* Which accounts receive the most focus](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/go-to-market-maturity-model-pillar-1-targeting-right-hallowell)
    - [*Messaging: **How salespeople are taught to engage prospects](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/go-to-market-maturity-model-pillar-2-messaging-stephen-hallowell)
    - [*Sales (& buying) process: **Key milestones and objectives in the customer engagement](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/go-to-market-maturity-model-pillar-3-sales-buying-stephen-hallowell/)
    - [*Accountability: **How leadership ensures consistent application of best practice](https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/go-to-market-maturity-model-pillar-4-accountability-stephen-hallowell/)

- It’s important to note that these pillars are multiplicative, not additive. That means you can’t try to max one of them out and move on to the next… if you have a zero anywhere in the system, you won’t get the output you want.!
