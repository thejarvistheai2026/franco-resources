# The Paths to Multi-Product

## Summary

Analysis of how successful SaaS companies expand from single-product to multi-product offerings, and the different strategic paths they take to achieve this growth.

## Key Framework

### Three Paths to Multi-Product

1. **Adjacent Products** — Building complementary tools for the same buyer
2. **Platform Expansion** — Opening infrastructure to third-party developers
3. **Upmarket/Downmarket** — Creating tiered offerings for different segments

### Critical Success Factors

- **Same buyer, new problem** — Easiest path to multi-product success
- **Shared data layer** — Products that leverage common infrastructure
- **Unified go-to-market** — Sales motion that can sell multiple products
- **Timing** — Single-product maturity before expansion

## Key Insights

- Multi-product companies trade at higher multiples
- The second product often determines long-term success
- Customer concentration risk decreases with product diversification
- Integration depth matters more than product breadth
