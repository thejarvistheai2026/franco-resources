#### Key Details

- for every object in your business... clients, projects, deliverables, team members

- i make them answer these three specific questions: 
	1. how do you CREATE one? 
	2. how do you MAINTAIN one? 
	3. how do you END one?

- you have processes for creation because that's exciting, that's growth but maintenance and endings? those run on autopilot in your brain scattered across Slack threads buried in "we'll figure it out" mentality the invisible processes are what's keeping you stuck example: client maintenance you probably don't have a system for: → when to send check-in emails → what triggers an account review → how you spot a client about to churn → when to upsell vs when to just deliver

- here's what we do: - map every business object (clients, projects, hires, deliverables) - document create/maintain/end for each one - find the invisible processes hiding in Slack and your brain - turn them into actual systems you can't automate what you can't see

- if you can't explain how you maintain a client relationship in 3 clear steps you don't have a process you have a mental habit and mental habits don't scale
