#### Summary
Based on the insight that 84% of the world has never used AI — the gap between what's possible and what people know = opportunity.

---

#### Core Insight
> "Most businesses haven't even started with AI. The gap between early adopters and everyone else is massive. And it's not closing anytime soon."

**Action:** Pick one path. Build one agent. Run one workshop. Automate one workflow. Help one group. Start now.

#### 1. AI Agent Setup Service
**Most direct path to revenue**

**Process:**
- Discovery call (30 min)
- Build custom agent for specific task
- Train on their data, tone, processes
- Set up automation triggers
- Deliver with docs + walkthrough

**Real examples:**
- Lead qualification agent (scores/routes inbound leads)
- Customer support agent (handles 80% of common questions)
- Content repurposing agent (1 video → 10 social posts)
- Meeting prep agent (researches attendees before calls)

**Pricing:**
- $2,000 basic single-workflow agent
- $5,000+ complex multi-step systems
- $99-250/month maintenance (recurring revenue)

**Tools:** Claude, Make, Zapier — no coding required

---

#### 2. Corporate AI Workshops
**What companies need after buying AI licenses they don't know how to use**

**Format (90 mins):**
- 15 min: Quick wins (prompt templates for their role)
- 30 min: Hands-on building (everyone creates workflow)
- 30 min: Department-specific use cases
- 15 min: Q&A + implementation planning

**Target:**
- Marketing teams (content, research, campaign planning)
- Sales teams (outreach, objection handling, proposals)
- Operations teams (docs, process improvement, reporting)
- Executive teams (strategic analysis, decision support)

**Pricing:**
- $500-1,500 single team session
- $5,000 full-day company workshop
- $2,500/month ongoing office hours

**Key:** Industry-specific beats generic. "AI for Real Estate Agents" \> "Intro to AI"

---

#### 3. Workflow Automation Systems
**Build once, sell to many**

**Formula:**
- Find painful, repetitive task every business in niche does
- Build automation once
- Package as done-for-you service with monthly maintenance

**Examples:**
- **Podcast content machine:** Raw episode → show notes, LinkedIn posts, short clips, X article, newsletter, blog post (24hr delivery)
- **Real estate listing agent:** Pulls property data → MLS description, social posts, email to buyers, tour script (saves 3 hrs/listing)
- **E-commerce content:** New SKUs → optimized titles, bullets, A+ content, ad copy (50 products/month)

**Leverage:** System runs for every client. Income scales without time scaling.

---

#### 4. Paid AI Community
**Long game that compounds**

**Structure:**
- Discord/Skool group
- Weekly live implementation calls
- Template library (prompts, workflows, agents)
- Member showcase + accountability

**Tiered pricing:**
- Base: $97/month (group, templates, weekly calls)
- Premium: $297/month (+ small group coaching, done-with-you builds, priority support, monthly 1:1s)

**Math:**
- 100 members @ $97 = $9,700/mo
- 20 premium @ $297 = $5,940/mo
- **Combined: $12,640/month** from teaching what you know
