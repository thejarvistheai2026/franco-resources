#### Summary
**The Prompt:**
> "What's the single smartest and most radically innovative and accretive and useful and compelling addition you could make to the plan at this point?"

**For existing projects:** Replace "plan" with "project"

#### Key Insight
From Jeffrey Emanuel: *"Yes, if you're not cranking the ambition factor to the max, you're wasting the potential of these frontier models. They've eclipsed us already, you just need to know how to draw it out of them."*

**Use case:** Run this prompt with multiple frontier models when you think you're "finished" with your agent development plan. The models often identify angles/ambitions you missed because they operate at higher capability ceilings.

**Why it works:** Explicitly asks for "radically innovative" and "accretive" additions — pushes model to stretch beyond incremental improvements into step-change territory.

#### How to Use
1. Finish your initial development plan/project documentation
2. Run this prompt across 2-3 frontier models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini)
3. Compare outputs — look for convergent suggestions (multiple models agree) and divergent ones (unique angles from specific models)
4. Incorporate the most compelling additions
