# How To Make $1 Million Per Year (Without Becoming An Employee)

## Summary

Alex Hormozi breaks down the fundamental shifts required to make $1M/year without being an employee — moving from trading time for money to trading value for money, understanding leverage, and solving bigger problems. The video covers four pillars: pricing, products, people, and platforms.

## Core Thesis

> "The fundamental reason that you're not making money is how you're thinking about making money."

**The employee mindset:** Time in → Money out. Trading hours for dollars.

**The entrepreneur mindset:** Value out → Money in. Trading solutions for dollars.

## The Four Pillars of $1M/Year

### Pillar 1: Pricing

**The $50/hour trap:**
- Work 2,000 hours/year
- Make $100K/year (upper middle class)
- **Problem:** No time left to scale

**The leverage shift:**
- Instead of selling hours, sell outcomes
- Package expertise into products
- Trade value for money, not time for money

| Model | Income | Hours | Lifestyle |
|-------|--------|-------|-----------|
| $50/hour service | $100K | 2,000 | Employee lifestyle |
| $5K product x 200 sales | $1M | Scalable | Freedom |

**Pricing psychology:**
- Charge based on value created, not time spent
- If you save someone $1M, charging $100K is reasonable
- If you help someone make $10M, charging $1M is reasonable

### Pillar 2: Products

**Service → Product → Portfolio**

1. **Service (trading time)** — Learn the skill
2. **Product (scaling)** — Package the skill
3. **Portfolio (diversification)** — Multiple products

**Product types:**
- **Info products:** Courses, coaching programs
- **Physical products:** E-commerce, manufacturing
- **Software:** SaaS, apps
- **Hybrid:** Done-with-you, done-for-you

**The transition:** Start with high-touch service, identify the repeatable pattern, productize it.

### Pillar 3: People

**The team equation:**
- You: Vision, strategy, capital allocation
- Team: Execution, delivery, operations

**Key hires (in order):**
1. **Delivery** — Free up your time from operations
2. **Sales** — Free up your time from acquisition
3. **Management** — Free up your time from managing

> "The goal is to do what only you can do, and hire for everything else."

**Delegation framework:**
- Document the process
- Train someone else
- Check their work
- Let them own it

### Pillar 4: Platforms

**Platform leverage:** Using existing infrastructure to scale

| Platform | Leverage Type |
|----------|---------------|
| YouTube | Content/attention |
| Social media | Distribution |
| Email | Direct access |
| Software | Automation |
| Marketplaces | Built-in demand |

**The compound effect:**
- Content works while you sleep
- Each piece builds on the last
- Platforms amplify reach exponentially

## The $1M Math

**$1M/year = $83K/month**

**Ways to get there:**
- $5K product × 17 sales/month
- $500 product × 167 sales/month
- $50 product × 1,667 sales/month
- $5K/month retainer × 17 clients
- $10K/month retainer × 8 clients

**The efficiency curve:** Higher price = fewer sales needed = less complexity

## Key Mindset Shifts

### 1. From Transactional to Transformational

**Employee:** What do I get for my time?
**Entrepreneur:** What value can I create?

### 2. From Consumer to Producer

**Consumer mindset:** Spending time and money
**Producer mindset:** Spending time to create value that generates money

### 3. From Active to Passive

**Active income:** Stop working = stop earning
**Passive income:** Systems work while you don't

**Reality check:** All income requires upfront work. "Passive" just means you did the work once and collect forever.

### 4. From Perfection to Iteration

> "Done is better than perfect."

- Launch before ready
- Let market feedback guide improvement
- Iterate in public

## The Skill Stack

**To make $1M/year, you need:**

| Skill | Application |
|-------|-------------|
| Marketing | Attract customers |
| Sales | Convert customers |
| Delivery | Keep customers |
| Operations | Scale delivery |
| Finance | Keep money |

**The learning loop:**
1. Pick one skill
2. Apply it
3. Learn from results
4. Repeat

## Common Traps

### Trap 1: The Certification Trap

Spending years getting credentials instead of building skills and serving customers.

### Trap 2: The Complexity Trap

Adding features/products before mastering one. Doing 10 things poorly vs. 1 thing excellently.

### Trap 3: The Scarcity Trap

Undercharging because of fear. Price is a filter for quality and commitment.

### Trap 4: The Solo Trap

Trying to do everything yourself. Hiring is how you scale beyond your personal capacity.

## The Path to $1M

**Phase 1: $0 → $10K/month (Learning)**
- Pick a skill
- Offer it as service
- Get 10 customers
- Learn what works

**Phase 2: $10K → $50K/month (Systematizing)**
- Document processes
- Package into product
- Build systems
- Hire first team member

**Phase 3: $50K → $200K/month (Scaling)**
- Add team
- Add products
- Add distribution
- Optimize operations

**Phase 4: $200K → $1M+/month (Investing)**
- Invest in other businesses
- Build portfolio
- Think in decades, not days

The goal isn't to work harder. It's to work on things that scale, build systems that compound, and solve problems worth paying for.
