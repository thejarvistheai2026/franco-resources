- Overly simplistic advice...
  “People don’t want to buy a 1/4” drill, they want a 1/4” hole.”
  Reality:
  Not aware ppl don’t buy 
  Problem aware buy holes 
  Solution aware buy drill bits 
  Product aware buy DeWalt
  Write your ad to address one. Write variants to address the others.

- Understanding your customer's pain points and benefits is not enough.
  To truly understand how they perceive your:
  - Differentiators 
  - Unique value 
  - Weaknesses 
  - Strengths 
  - You need to learn about the alternative solutions they currently use to solve their pain.
