# The Cadence: How to Operate a SaaS Startup

## Summary

Craft Ventures' framework for operating SaaS startups through a regular cadence of meetings, metrics reviews, and strategic planning — designed to create rhythm and predictability in high-growth companies.

## The Cadence Framework

### Daily

- **Standups** — Blockers, progress, priorities
- **Customer activity review** — Support tickets, churn signals

### Weekly

- **Metrics review** — Core KPIs, trends, anomalies
- **Team leads sync** — Cross-functional alignment
- **Product/customer feedback** — Voice of customer

### Monthly

- **Board-level metrics** — Full financial and operational review
- **Strategic initiatives** — Progress on key projects
- **Team health check** — Morale, capacity, blockers

### Quarterly

- **Board meeting** — Full strategic review
- **OKR setting** — Objectives and key results
- **Planning cycle** — Resource allocation for next quarter

## Key Principles

- **Rhythm creates predictability** — Teams perform better with known cycles
- **Metrics drive behavior** — What you measure is what you optimize
- **Cadence scales** — Same framework from 10 to 1000 people

## Key Insights

- Most startups under-invest in operational cadence early
- The right meeting rhythm reduces total meeting time
- Monthly metrics reviews catch problems before they become crises
