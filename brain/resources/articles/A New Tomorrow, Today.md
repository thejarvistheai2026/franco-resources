#### Key Details

- **A year ago, if someone asked for “cheap, fast, and good,” you’d tell them to pick two. Today, with the right mix of tools and talent, all three are possible, and that shift has now reached client expectations.**
- **Creative output will become increasingly commoditized through AI’s generative capabilities in image, text design, and code. 
- This means that the new moat in creativity is architecture—creative direction, principles that define a brand, and the systems that guide outputs at scale.**
