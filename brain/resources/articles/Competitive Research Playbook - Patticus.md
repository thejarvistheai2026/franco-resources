# Competitive Research Playbook

## Summary

A tactical guide to conducting competitive research for product and pricing decisions, with frameworks for analyzing competitors and translating insights into action.

## The Playbook

### Phase 1: Discovery

- Identify direct and indirect competitors
- Map the competitive landscape
- Find "hidden" competitors (substitutes, DIY solutions)

### Phase 2: Analysis

- **Feature comparison** — Functional capabilities
- **Pricing analysis** — Models, tiers, packaging
- **Positioning audit** — Messaging, target segments
- **Customer research** — Reviews, forums, win/loss data

### Phase 3: Synthesis

- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Determine differentiation strategy
- Update positioning and pricing

## Key Frameworks

### The Value Matrix

Map competitors on:
- **X-axis:** Price (low to high)
- **Y-axis:** Value delivered (low to high)

### The Jobs-to-be-Done Lens

Analyze what "job" customers hire competitors to do, not just feature lists.

## Key Insights

- Competitive research is continuous, not one-time
- Your real competitors may not be who you think
- Pricing research requires actual purchase intent data
- Win/loss analysis is the highest-ROI research
