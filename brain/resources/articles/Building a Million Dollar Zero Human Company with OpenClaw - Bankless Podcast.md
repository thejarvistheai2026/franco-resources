## Summary

Bankless podcast episode featuring Nat Eliason discussing how he built a "zero human company" using OpenClaw and Felix Craft AI — an AI CEO that ships products, runs operations, and manages other agents for support and sales. The business (Claw Mart) deploys custom OpenClaw employees for real businesses and has hit nearly $80k in revenue since early February with surprisingly low monthly costs.

## Key Thesis/Insights

- **Zero human company**: AI CEO (Felix) that ships products, runs ops, manages support/sales agents
- **Claw Mart**: Service deploying custom OpenClaw employees for real businesses
- **Revenue**: Nearly $80k since early February 2026
- **Low operational costs**: Surprisingly low monthly costs compared to traditional staffing
- **From info product to marketplace**: Started as overnight info product, evolved into full marketplace
